About Our Initiative

Women's safety is an urgent and ongoing societal concern. Despite technological advancements and increased awareness, incidents of harassment and threats persist in both urban and rural contexts.

This project proposes the development of a Women Safety Alert Website — a secure, real-time platform designed to empower women with rapid emergency alert systems, safety resources, location sharing, and community support features.
